This website is about hamburgers.

Ingredients

The ingredients needed are meat and bread. You can add on; pickles,kectchup,mustard.

Method

The method is to place the meat on the grill grate and cook, waiting for at least 4 minutes before flipping. Cook until both sides are browned and the meat is cooked to the desired doneness, about 10 minutes total for medium-rare. Assemble the hamburgers.Let the hamburgers rest for about 5 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
